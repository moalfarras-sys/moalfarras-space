Why I started this channel

The channel started with a simple idea: Arabic tech content that feels real, filmed by someone who actually lives with the devices instead of just reading the box.

I focus on gear that fits everyday life: cleaning devices, home tools, screens, audio and small gadgets that make the day a bit easier. When I talk about a product, I try to highlight what I wish someone had told me before buying it.

How do I choose what to review?

  • Devices I already use at home or really need.
  • Products people keep asking about in comments and messages.
  • Things that can save time, effort or money for people in similar situations.
